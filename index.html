About Us

Reducing plastic waste, one meal at a time.

EcoWare Solutions is a dedicated supplier of biodegradable and compostable tableware, serving wholesale buyers across the country. We noticed that many businesses wanted to switch away from single-use plastics but struggled to find reliable, high-volume suppliers. That is why we built a supply chain focused entirely on plant-based food packaging.

Today, we supply restaurants, event organizers, and distributors with sturdy, reliable products made from natural materials like sugarcane and birchwood.

Our Mission

"To help food service businesses transition to sustainable packaging by providing reliable, high-quality, and affordable compostable products."
